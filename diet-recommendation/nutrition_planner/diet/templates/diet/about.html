How It Works

Nutrition Planner uses scientifically proven formulas to create personalized meal plans based on your unique health data and goals.

1. Calculate Your BMR

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, considered the most accurate formula for calculating Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR):

Men: BMR = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5
Women: BMR = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161
2. Calculate Your TDEE

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is calculated by multiplying your BMR by an activity factor:

  • Sedentary: BMR × 1.2 (little or no exercise)
  • Lightly Active: BMR × 1.375 (light exercise 1-3 days/week)
  • Moderately Active: BMR × 1.55 (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week)
  • Very Active: BMR × 1.725 (hard exercise 6-7 days/week)
  • Extra Active: BMR × 1.9 (very hard exercise & physical job)
3. Adjust for Your Goal

We adjust your calorie target based on your goal:

  • Lose Weight: TDEE - 500 calories/day
  • Maintain Weight: TDEE (no adjustment)
  • Gain Weight: TDEE + 500 calories/day
4. Calculate Macro Targets

Macronutrients are distributed based on your goal:

  • Weight Loss: 40% Protein, 30% Carbs, 30% Fat
  • Maintain: 30% Protein, 40% Carbs, 30% Fat
  • Weight Gain: 30% Protein, 50% Carbs, 20% Fat
5. Generate Personalized Meal Plans

Our intelligent meal generator creates daily meal plans that match your calorie and macro targets, while respecting your dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, keto, etc.).
